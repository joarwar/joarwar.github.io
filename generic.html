What is LIA?

Lärande i Arbete (LIA) is an internship for students who studies at Teknikhögskolan. LIA is the workplace-based part of the education and is there to give the student a deeper insight and practically rooted competence. In the education, it is seen as a course with course objectives that should assessed on the basis of performance
